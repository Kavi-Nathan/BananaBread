# Banana Bread Recipie



## Wet Ingredients
 3 bananas
 3/4 cups Sugar 
 1/2 cup flavorless oil
 1 tsp Vanilla Extract 

 ## Dry Ingredients
 3/4 cups All Purpose Flour 
 3/4 cup Wheat Flour
 1 tbsp Cinnamon Powder
 1 tsp Baking Powder
 2 tsp Baking Soda

 1/2 cup Walnuts
 1/4 cup of chocolate chips
